Position Summary:

This position focuses on the role of a front-end web designer that helps define and design exceptional user experiences for new responsive efforts as well as improvements to existing digital experiences. You also would be responsible for website enhancements or updates and help implement ongoing conversions to responsive design. In addition you would help define and design exceptional user experiences for new responsive efforts and improvements to existing digital experiences. You must be able to translate business requirements and end-user goals into visually appealing and easy-to-use digital designs, wireframes, interactive prototypes, while leveraging brand and digital standards.

Specific responsibilities of this position include:

  • Work collaboratively with the business to understand and verify business, functional and technical requirements needed to support development of technology and/or business-driven technology solutions.
  • Interpret or explain data or information to deliver messages to audiences not knowledgeable about the subject matter.
  • Innovate and iterate based on feedback as well as ability to work collaboratively as part of a team.
  • Build websites and webpages using responsive design. This includes being able to understand current standards, including cross-browser styling and optimizations (Chrome, Firefox, Internet Explorer, Safari.)
  • Develop prototypes using prototyping wireframe packages
  • Provide deep expertise with current front-end technologies: HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
  • Stay up-to-date on emerging technologies
  • Ability to work well in both functional and cross-functional team environments, often under the pressure of tight deadlines and high expectations
  • Assist with the testing and implementation of websites and webpages to ensure successful installation of changes
  • Provide on-call support to production websites

Position Qualifications

  • Degree in web or computer related field.
  • Three to five years of web design or development experience required.
  • Certification in usability and ADA standards a plus.
  • Portfolio of design accomplishments required.
  • Excellent communication skills with a customer focus
  • Strong consulting skills with attention to detail.
  • High proficiency with Adobe Creative Suite.
